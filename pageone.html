The Bus Open Data Service (BODS) provides bus Location, Fares and Timetables data for every local bus service in England. In order to make this possible, operators within scope need to publish their Location, Timetables and Fares data onto BODS.
Please note that the deadline and grace period for uploading bus data has now passed, and enforcement may start against those who are non-compliant.
